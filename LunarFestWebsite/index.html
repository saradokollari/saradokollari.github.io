ABOUT LUNAR FEST

Festival Intro:

Lunar Fest is a three-day celebration of sound, light, and connection, bringing together music lovers from around the world to dance under the summer night sky. Set along the coast of Los Angeles, Lunar Fest 2026 features world-class artists, immersive art installations, and unforgettable energy from sunset ‘til dawn.

Lineup & Experience:

From global superstars like The Weeknd, Doja Cat, and Zedd, to fan favorites like Twice, Chappell Roan, and Ashnikko, the lineup spans genres and cultures for a one-of-a-kind music experience. Each night brings a new wave of sound: pop, EDM, indie, and beyond, all glowing beneath the moonlight.

“Three Nights of Music Under the Stars.”
